Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This is a quick and easy grilled cheese sandwich made with white bread and cheddar cheese

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  • Preheat skillet over medium heat and generously butter one slice of bread.
  • Place butter side down on skillet and top with slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread and place butter side up on top of the cheese.
  • Cook until lightly browned on one side, flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  • Repeat all steps above with the other half of the ingredients.
