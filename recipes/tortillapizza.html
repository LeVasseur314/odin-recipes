Easy Tortilla Pizza

Description

This is a quick and easy light snack pizza you can make and switch up the toppings to however you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 inch) soft flour tortilla
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons tomato sauce
  • 1 cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pinch dried oregano

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  • Place tortilla on a baking sheet. Brush with olive oil; sprinkle garlic powder, salt, and pepper on top.
  • Bake in oven for about 3 to 5 minutes. Remove from oven and top with tomato sauce, chicken, bell peppers, green onions, and then cheese.
  • Bake in oven another 5 minutes. Sprinkle with oregano and cut pizza into fourths.
