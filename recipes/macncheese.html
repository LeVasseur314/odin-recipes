Simple Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a quick and easy alternative to the cheap box macaroni and the expensive restaurant macaroni

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  • Bring pot of salted water to boil and cook macaroni in the water, stirring occasionally until cooked through, about 8 minutes.
  • Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. add flour salt and pepper and stir until smooth for about 5 minutes. Slowly add milk while stirring. Keep heating and stirring until mixture is bubbling, about 5 minutes.
  • Add cheese and ook until melted, about 2 to 4 minutes.
  • Drain macaroni and mix into the sauce.
